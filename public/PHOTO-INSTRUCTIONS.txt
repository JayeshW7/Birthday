HOW TO ADD THE BIRTHDAY PHOTO
================================

To add the birthday person's photo:

1. Place your photo file in the /public folder
2. Rename it to: photo-placeholder.jpg
   (or update the filename in pages/[...name].js if you prefer a different name)

3. Supported formats: .jpg, .jpeg, .png, .webp

The photo will appear in a circular frame on the birthday wish page!

Current location for photo: /public/photo-placeholder.jpg
